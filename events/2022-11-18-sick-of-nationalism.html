• Sick of Nationalism. Audience based durational performance. 18/11 2022 16pm-22pm at The National Gallery of Arts in Denmark.
  • A circle of chairs in the middle of the exhibition room of Danish and Nordic Arts 1750-1900 at SMK Friday, Sindets sansninger, 18/11 2022, 16pm-22pm at The National Gallery of Arts in Denmark. On each chair is a copy of the script - circular with no beginning or end point. There is a microphone in front of each chair picking up the voices of the spectactors. The spectactors can enter and leave the dialogue whenever they want, they improvise their ways together.
